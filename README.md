## What is Jobmeter?

Jobmeter is a modern job board designed with a mobile-first experience, helping candidates quickly find relevant roles without unnecessary friction.

We focus on:

- Fresh, daily job listings  
- Clean and fast user experience  
- Scam awareness and job transparency  
- Smart filtering by role, sector, and location  
- Remote and hybrid opportunities  

Unlike cluttered job sites, Jobmeter prioritizes **speed, clarity, and accessibility.**
